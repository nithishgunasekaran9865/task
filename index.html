Welcome to

Dr.Ruchi’s MultispecialityDentalCare

‘Invest in your smile, it’s the first thing people notice about you.’

Dr. Ruchi’s Dental Care is a multispecialty dental clinic located in Coimbatore, offering a comprehensive range of dental treatments to patients of all ages. The clinic is well-equipped with state-of-the-art technology and a team of highly trained and experienced dental professionals, led by Dr. Ruchi herself.


At Dr. Ruchi’s Dental Care, patients can receive a wide range of dental treatments, including:

  • Orthodontic treatment, including braces, aligners, and other orthodontic appliances

  • Cosmetic dentistry, including teeth whitening, veneers, and dental bonding

  • Restorative dentistry, including dental implants, crowns, bridges, and dentures

  • Pediatric dentistry, including preventive care, fillings, and extractions

  • Oral surgery, including extractions, root canal therapy, and gum surgery

  • Periodontics, including scaling and root planing, gum surgery, and other treatments for gum disease
